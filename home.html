Eskisehir is a city in northwestern Turkey and the capital of the Eskisehir Province. The urban population of the city is 717,135 with a metropolitan population of 826,135.

University Street

The city is located on the banks of the Porsuk River, 792 m above sea level, where it overlooks the fertile Phrygian Valley. In the nearby hills one can find hot springs.

The city is 233 km (145 mi) to the west of Ankara, 330 km (205 mi) to the southeast of Istanbul and 78 km (48 mi) to the northeast of Kutahya.
